Prompt
Use this document as a means of documenting your exploration of the Summer Project, the slides are headed in the format we will be using for the academic year. Please use a combination of text and images to demonstrate your journey and processes explored in creating the boxes. - you may also choose to use audio or videos to support the work.
The body of work presented should show your thought process and reflections as well as the final outcomes. The purpose of the the Foundation Course is to fully embed an exploratory sense of process - it is the journey that’s important not the outcome!

Design
